There are 3 main juggling props. The balls can range from 60-90mm in diameter and 70-110g in weight. Most of them have a seed filling with a 4-panel sewing. The clubs consist of a body and handle, and are a great way to step-up your juggling! The rings are, well rings! Made with plastic and love 😀

Russian balls have an outer plastic shell, with sand, salt or grain inside. The unique weight gives a more stable direction when thrown and is great for balancing tricks. The thud balls are a bit smaller and lighter, coming in at 70g and 62mm. They don't roll away when dropped, but rather make a 'thud'!
